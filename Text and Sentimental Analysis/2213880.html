TED Talks by Speaker Gabby Giffords and Speaker Jessica Ladd

Banini, Obed

Introduction

This report seeks to present and compare two TED Talk speakers by name Gabby Giffords and Jessica Ladd through text analytics and sentiment analytics. Gabby Giffords in 2014 spoke on the topic: Be passionate. Be courageous. Be your best. Jessica Ladd in 2016 spoke on the topic is titled: The reporting system that sexual assault survivors want. This report will try to project the words and sentiments (positive and negative) and also compare the relationship in terms of choice of words and sentiments shared by the speakers.

Methods

Firstly, the process will start cleaning the data, removing stopwords to help focus on the main vocabulary of speakers to gain insight. Secondly, the both speakers will be filtered out individually for descriptive analysis to made then plot against each other to determine if vocabulary used by both speakers are mutual in any way. This process will be followed by a sentiment analysis using the bing and nrc lexicon to gain various insights. Descriptive statistics, Data Exploration, Correlation analysis and odds ratio summary statistics are the statistical summary measures that will be used to compare results, and the visualization that will produced are scatter plots, bar charts and frequency tables.
